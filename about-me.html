About me

Namaste! I am a User experience researcher with a bachelor's in design from IIT Guwahati. I enjoy collaborating with multidisciplinary teams on solving challenging problems and enhancing the user experience of products.

Currently, I'm leading the research for the User acquisition and engagement domain focused on Videos, Gamification, and User-generated content at Flipkart. Also, I spearheaded the research for Voice user interfaces and the Grocery category in e-commerce.

Previously, I worked as a product designer to improve the post-purchase in the shopping journey and redesign the news section in a digital news company.
